Veggie Puttanesca

Description

Make a veggie version of the classic puttanesca!

Ingredients

Serves two

  • 150g spaghetti or tagliatelle
  • Clove of garlic (chopped)
  • One onion (chopped)
  • Black olives (chopped)
  • Capers
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Stock cube
  • Veggie parmesan (grated)
  • Tablespoon of olive oil

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a pan of salted boiling water and follow the packet instructions.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan, then gently heat onion for ten minutes. Then add the chopped garlic.
  3. Add chopped tomatoes, olives, capers and stock cube. Heat on medium, stirring regularly.
  4. Drain pasta and serve topped with sauce and veggie parmesan.
