Tomato Pasta Recipe

Description

Deliciously simple meal, made from storecupboard ingredients.

Ingredients

Serves two

  • 150g penne pasta
  • Clove of garlic
  • Tin of chopped tomatoes
  • Tablespoon of basil (frozen or dried)
  • Tablespoon of parsley (dried)
  • Pinch of sugar
  • Salt and pepper
  • Cheddar (grated)
  • Chilli flakes (optional)

Steps

  1. Put the pasta in a pan of salted boiling water and follow the packet instructions.
  2. Put all the remaining ingredients in a food blender and blend to make tomato sauce.
  3. Put tomato sauce in a pan and heat on medium, season.
  4. Drain pasta and add to tomato sauce pan. Stir.
  5. Serve with grated cheddar and optional chilli flakes
